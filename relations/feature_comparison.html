EVALUATION OF FEATURES FOR 2nd STAGE CLASSIFIER

PART 1.
Conclusions of evaluating different feature configurations.
  • Dense sampling and more pyramid levels are complementary
  • Dense sampling is particularly costly for ColorSIFT
  • ColorSIFT always performed worse than regular SIFT
  • See images ranked by classifier scores

PART 2.
Replacing ColorSIFT by Spatial Pyramids of Color Histograms.
  • ColorSIFT is too expensive to compute and performs worse than regular SIFT
  • We can replace local color information of ColorSIFT with SP of RGB histograms
  • It results in the same performance under much cheaper computation
  • See images ranked by classifier scores
